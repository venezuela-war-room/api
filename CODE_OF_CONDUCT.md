# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want this project to be safe, respectful, and useful for contributors, volunteers, maintainers, and people affected by the earthquake in Venezuela.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and specific.
- Prioritize the safety and dignity of affected people.
- Protect private or sensitive information.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project and helping people.
- Be patient with volunteers and contributors working under urgency.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Sharing sensitive disaster-relief data in public issues, PRs, logs, screenshots, or examples.
- Exploiting project data or affected communities for personal gain.
- Repeated disruption of maintainer decisions or emergency-response workflows.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues or pull requests, and restrict participation when behavior creates risk for contributors, users, maintainers, or affected communities.
